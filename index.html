    The Walkways

    Enjoy the panoramic views of London from the Tower of London to St Pauls in the west, and Canary Wharf to Shooter’s Hill in the east, experience the London skyline from a unique perspective above the Thames. Then look down and see London life through the Glass Floors, from a unique viewpoint 42 meters above the river. And if you’re lucky. enjoy the magic of the Bridge lifting below your feet!
